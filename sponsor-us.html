How To Sponsor Us

Why sponsor our Team?

Our students have a deep passion for STEM. By sponsoring our team, you help us achieve our goals of learning new skills and deepening our learning and love for robotics. Additionally, you are helping to increase the number of kids pursuing college degrees and longer-term careers in STEM fields.

How does your sponsorship help our team?

Your support helps to fund all the expenses needed for us to function as a team. Your sponsorship will go towards our workshop, purchasing tools and robot materials needed to design, build, and compete. Your sponsorship also allows us to provide opportunities for us to learn new skills such as CAD and programming. Most importantly, it allows us also to give back to our communities and help other kids find and deepen their passions for robotics.

What are the benefits of sponsorship?

We will advertise for your company at events where approximately 500+ current and future STEM professionals attend by showcasing your logo in many different ways on our robot, team t-shirts, booth banner, website, and other promotional materials. We will also share information with our partners and fellow FTC colleagues about your organization and how your support makes a difference for aspiring tech students!

Sponsorship Levels:

Stealth Title - Donation of $10,000 or more
Stealth Platinum - Donation of $5,000 - $9,999
Stealth Gold - Donation between $1,000 - $4,999
Stealth Silver - Donations up to $1,000

Other ways to support the team?

In addition to sponsoring our team, you can also partner with us by allowing us to tour your company to learn more about your organization and products, and connect us to a professional in your organization that can teach our team something new that we can apply to our team, robot, or community, offer intern or job shadowing opportunities, and spread the word about our team to other organizations who may be interested in sponsoring us.

Our commitment to you

We are committed to deepening our love and passion for robotics, inspiring the next generation of students to do the same, and finding ways we can make a difference to give back to communities and under-resourced teams.

How do I make a donation?

All donations are tax-deductible. Stealth Robotics is a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit based in Illinois. Tax-deductible donations should be written to Stealth Robotics. If you would like to sponsor our team, use our Contact Us page to get in touch with us.
