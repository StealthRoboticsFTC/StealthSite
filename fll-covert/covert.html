About Our Team:

Covert is the official FIRST LEGO League (FLL) team under Stealth Robotics, focused on inspiring young minds to explore STEM through teamwork and creativity.

What is FLL?

FIRST LEGO League is a global robotics program for elementary and middle school students. Teams design, build, and program LEGO-based robots to complete themed challenges that encourage problem-solving, innovation, and teamwork.

Covert's Mission

At Covert, we aim to:

  • Build technical and collaborative skills in a fun, hands-on environment.
  • Empower students to tackle real-world problems using robotics and innovation.
  • Foster a passion for STEM while creating lifelong learning opportunities.
