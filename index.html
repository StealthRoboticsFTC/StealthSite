Team Mission

We are a group of aspiring engineers committed to deepening our love and passion for robotics, inspiring the next generation of students to do the same, and finding ways we can make a difference to give back to communities and under-resourced teams. We are team #18255, part of a non-for-profit 501(c)3 organization.

Focus Areas:

  • Stealth Students - Our team includes six students representing four local schools, Elgin Academy, Barrington High School , Jacobs High School, and Johnsburg High School.
  • Stealth Community - Our team is passionate about teaching robotics. We hope to mentor other teams where we can teach others new skills and share our experiences.
  • Stealth Experiences - Our team is “hands-on team”. We gain new experiences and learn by getting our hands dirty, learning from our mistakes, and constantly trying new ways of doing things.

"Most of the time you will fail, but you will also occasionally succeed. Those occasional successes make all the hard work and sacrifice worthwhile."

Dean Kamen – Founder and President of FIRST
