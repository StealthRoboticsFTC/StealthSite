About Our Team:

We are a group of aspiring engineers committed to deepening our love and passion for robotics, inspiring the next generation of students to do the same, and finding ways to give back to our communities and support under-resourced teams. We are Team #18255, part of a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization.

Focus Areas:

  • Stealth Students - Our team includes students representing local schools: Barrington High School, Prairie Middle School, and Burlington Central High School.
  • Stealth Community - We are passionate about teaching robotics. We mentor other teams, share our knowledge, and help others develop new skills.
  • Stealth Experiences - We are a hands-on team. We gain new experiences by getting our hands dirty, learning from our mistakes, and constantly trying new ways of doing things.

Our Goals:

  • Foster Innovation: By constantly experimenting with new ideas and technologies, we strive to be at the forefront of innovation in robotics.
  • Build Leaders: We believe in developing leadership skills within our team, empowering each member to take initiative, make decisions, and lead by example.
  • Promote STEM Education: We are passionate advocates for STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education. Through workshops, mentorship, and outreach programs, we encourage young students to pursue careers in these fields.

Join Us:

Are you interested in supporting our journey? Whether you're a student eager to learn, a mentor with valuable experience, or a company looking to sponsor a local robotics team, there are many ways to get involved with Team #18255.

  • Become a Sponsor: Your sponsorship helps us cover the costs of materials, tools, and competition fees. In return, we proudly showcase your support on our robots, team uniforms, and promotional materials at events attended by hundreds of STEM professionals and students.
  • Partner with Us: We're always looking for opportunities to collaborate with local businesses and organizations. Whether it's offering internships, job shadowing, or hosting a team tour of your facilities, your partnership can make a big impact on our educational journey.
  • Mentor or Volunteer: If you have expertise in robotics, programming, engineering, or any related field, consider joining us as a mentor or volunteer. Your guidance can help shape the next generation of innovators.

Our Commitment:

  • Deepening our passion for robotics.
  • Inspiring the next generation of STEM leaders.
  • Making a difference by giving back to our communities and supporting under-resourced teams.
